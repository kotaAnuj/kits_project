Using Credits

Digital solar allows you to own a part of a solar project without installing panels on your roof. You get credits for the power produced, which reduce your electricity bill.

Our digital solar projects are installed in various locations, typically large-scale solar farms, to maximize efficiency and output.

No, you do not need any approval from your DISCOM. Our service works by providing you with credits that offset your bill, not by directly connecting to your home's electricity supply.

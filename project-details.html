About the Project

Kepler•100 marks the fifth digital solar project from JRP Eco Solutions, and the first one in the series to be situated in Navi Mumbai. This project consists of 100 kW capacity of Bifacial Mono-PERC modules built on the rooftops of Arihant Aradhana CHS - A residential complex of two wings comprising 17 storeys each.

Every unit (kWh) from this project discounts ₹6.42 off your power bill
